I worked with [Shiying Li](https://shiyinglive.github.io/){:target="_blank"} and other faculty at UNL to develop the [SPARK data science camp](https://4h.unl.edu/statewide-events-opportunities/big-red-summer-academic-camps/camp-sessions/#tab-item-math-&-data-science-camp-3538){:target="_blank"}, a one week summer camp for high school students on the mathematics of data science.  Under the guidance of Shiying, I created slides, worksheets, and coding exercises for the students.  These materials were then refined by the camp's committee, which I served on for one year.  In June 2026, I led the SPARK camp and taught 13 students about data science.
